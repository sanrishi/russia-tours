## 1. Project Overview

Development of a custom, multi-page tourism website targeting Indian travelers interested in guided tours to Russia. Built using a modern React-based architecture with a focus on maintainability, performance, and scalability.

The project follows a **Strategy + Execution** approach — every design and UX decision is made with the goal of converting visitors into booking enquiries.

### Design References

The visual and structural direction is based on the four reference sites you provided:

- **discover.moscow** — Visual design language, card-based layouts, bold typography
- **russia.tours** — Tour package structure, pricing display, testimonial patterns
- **discoverrussia.travel** — "Plan your Trip" module structure (Visa, Safety, Transport)
- **discoverrussia.ru/en** — Cultural storytelling and brand tone
